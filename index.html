About Us

Skipperz Club Nairobi is a youth-led community promoting fitness, wellness, and fun through rope skipping. We organize free sessions, challenges, and community events to keep Nairobi active, healthy, and inspired.

Our Activities

  • Skipping Challenges & Competitions
  • Stretching & Warm-Up Sessions
  • Wellness Talks & Health Workshops
  • Dance & Skip Jams
  • Community Fun Days for Kids & Families
